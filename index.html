CEIE

CEIE (Controlled Environment Integration Engine) is a comprehensive, modular platform designed to standardize and automate version management and CI/CD workflows.

Whether you are configuring repositories, scripting deployments, or managing releases, CEIE provides a cohesive toolset to enhance productivity and reliability across your development lifecycle.
